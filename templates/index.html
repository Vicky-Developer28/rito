Project Overview & Vision

India stands at a pivotal moment in its digital transformation. With over 954.40 million internet users, the digital economy is a primary engine of growth and innovation. However, this rapid expansion has occurred on a foundation built and controlled by foreign entities.

The Problem: Systemic Vulnerabilities

  • Lack of Sovereignty: Inability to control core security updates independently
  • Fragmented Security: Security implemented at application level on insecure OS
  • Identity and Accountability Crisis: Epidemic of identity theft and financial fraud
  • Hardware Dependency: Reliance on global semiconductor vendors

Our Vision: A Sovereign Digital India

We envision a digital India where every citizen can interact online with confidence, privacy, and security. A nation that controls its digital destiny, from the silicon of its devices to the software that powers its services.

The Rito System is not just an identity solution; it is a holistic re-architecting of India's digital public infrastructure, creating an ecosystem of trust where technology serves the citizen and the nation.

System Architecture

The Rito System establishes a verifiable "chain of trust" from the device hardware up, mandating accountability for digital platforms and providing robust tools to protect vulnerable citizens.

Core Technical Components

The Rito System integrates multiple sovereign components to create a comprehensive digital identity framework.

Rito Digital Identity

A cryptographically secured digital credential bound to a user and their specific, certified device.

  • Root of Trust: Device's ARM-based TEE
  • Key Storage: Asymmetric key pair
  • Biometric Data: Encrypted, device-local templates

Igris OS

A sovereign, security-hardened desktop OS for user devices with privacy-by-default design.

  • Base: Fork of FreeBSD for stability & security
  • Verified Boot & Hardware Attestation
  • Mandatory Application Sandboxing

Aegis OS

A privacy-first mobile operating system with enhanced security controls and Rito integration.

  • Base: Hardened fork of Android Open Source Project
  • Enhanced Permission System
  • Hardware-Backed Keystore

Shaga OS

A sovereign, high-performance server OS for national infrastructure with verifiable security.

  • Jail-Based Isolation for services
  • Formal Verification of critical components
  • Built-in DDoS Mitigation

Rito Protocol

A distributed authentication and verification system using Zero-Knowledge Proofs and permissioned blockchain.

  • Zero-Knowledge Proof Engine
  • Federated Blockchain Identity Layer
  • Multi-Factor Authentication Framework

IEDA

Indian Electrical Device Auth - A hardware identification system for all internet-connectable devices.

  • Hardware chip integrated into all ICDs
  • 128-bit globally unique identifier
  • Secure storage preventing tampering
